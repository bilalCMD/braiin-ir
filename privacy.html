Privacy Policy

How Braiin Limited collects, uses, and protects your information on this Investor Relations website.

Last updated: 11 June 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Braiin Limited ("Braiin", "we", "us") handles personal information collected through this Investor Relations website (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

2. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: name, email address, company, and phone number when you submit an enquiry form or subscribe to email alerts.
  • Automatically collected: basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, used to keep the Site secure and improve performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and shareholder service requests.
  • To send investor alerts and updates you have subscribed to.
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and performance of the Site.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

4. How We Protect Your Data

All traffic to this Site is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards — including access controls, multi-factor authentication, and encrypted storage — to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate the Site (for example, hosting and email-delivery providers), or where required by law or regulation.

6. Cookies

The Site may use essential cookies and similar technologies to function correctly and to understand aggregate usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, or as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, and to unsubscribe from communications at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Third-Party Links

This Site links to third-party sources such as the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and stock-market data providers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact our Investor Relations team at BRAI@mzgroup.us or visit our Contact page.
