About Us

Intelligence for the physical world.

Braiin Limited (NASDAQ: BRAI) is a global AI platform company delivering automation and predictive analytics across precision agriculture, customer experience, and property technology.

The Company's platforms combine autonomous robotics, IoT, and machine learning to modernize analogue industries. Headquartered in Western Australia, operations span seven countries with ~800 employees.
